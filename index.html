Gon

The Gon believe that their ancestors were born from the breath of Dragons and they embrace this legend with pride.

Physical power and combative skill are treasured among the Gon. They seek to make themselves strong, capable, and able to overcome any challenge, and use their might for the good of others. This can make Gon somewhat easy to trick, as the greedy use the Gon's intimidating physique and trusting nature for personal gain. However, if you are true, friendships established with Gon are as strong as steel and you would have earned their loyalty for life.

In their day to day lives, Gon move and speak at a calm and measured pace. This deliberate pacing was started to help nullify the other Gon trait: their famously short tempers. With their massive frame and incredible strength, little can stand in the way of these mighty people once a Gon has been moved to anger.

Available Classes: Destroyer, Kung Fu Master, Force Master, Soul Fighter, Warden

Jin

The word "Jin" means "Unyielding Effort" in the ancient language of the eastern continent. It is this characteristic that still defines the Jin people, who make up most of the world's most celebrated people.

Jin warriors may look fairly ordinary, and are certainly smaller than their Gon counterparts, but beneath a somewhat plain exterior lays incredible strength and tenacity, typical of the heirs of the Black Tortoise. Their versatility and never-say-die attitude gives Jin warriors a reputation that few would risk testing in the battlefield.

Lyn

The Lyn, with their large ears and dainty tails, are descendants from the Kirin, legendary animals that were both mischievous and unpredictable

The Lyn have not survived in this dangerous world by mere chance; they have innately acute senses which steer them clear from danger and allow them to see treachery and deception where others cannot. More famously, the Lyn use these highly attuned senses to converse with beings from the Spirit Realm granting them the ability to commune with Familiars and harness the power of the earth.

The Lyn are known for their somewhat unpredictable nature. During a single conversation, it is not unusual for a Lyn to convey a multitude of emotions, including hatred, fear, jealousy, love, and respect.

Available Classes: Summoner, Force Master, Blade Dancer, Warlock, Gunslinger, Warden.

Destroyer
Difficult:
Tribe: Gon
Element: Earth / Dark

True to its name, The Destroyer possesses the strongest destructive skill of Blade and Soul. Large body size often makes them the main target of the enemy attack. However, no matter how aggressive the enemy may be, The Destroyer can easily eliminate those who are trying to stop them.

The characteristic of The Destroyer lies in the ability to capture the enemy, overpower the enemy and subdue them before their invincible power. They also use fallen opponents as weapons to attack other enemies, keeping their teammates safe.

Force Master
Difficult:
Tribe: Yun, Lyn, Gon
Element: Fire / Ice

The Force Master is a sect that controls the natural elements at its will: Fire Awesome with the ability to accumulate damage and attack continuously; or Great Ice with the ability to hold down enemies and strengthen defenses. At a high level, Force Master can bind enemies in mid-air, making them targets of attacks that cannot be defended.

The ability to deal large-scale damage puts the Force Master at number one in the sect of destroying enemy groups, but the lack of melee capability makes PvP battles with the Force Master a challenge. A good disciple of the Force Master will have to work hard to balance this situation.

Blade Dancer
Difficult:
Tribe: Lyn
Element: Lightning / Wind

Blade Dancer is known for his rapid, transformational, and wide area attacks in place of defensive skills. Blade Dancer moves at an incredible speed, giving the opponent no chance to attack. With special physique and mysterious techniques, the Blade Dancer can take down enemies quickly in their powerlessness.

The quick and continuous blows helped the Blade Dancer to overcome the duels, while the wide control skills made them key members of the squad.

Assassin
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin
Element: Dark / Lightning

The Assassin drains the enemy's life force with lightning attacks, poisoning and making the wound bleed nonstop. The most powerful secret of the Assassins is the virtual moves that make them invisible to the enemies, appearing on the battlefield and even exchanging positions with the opponent.

Among all factions, Assassin is most powerful when alone. However, these sect's continuous transformation and damage techniques are still extremely important to major battles.

Blade Master
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin, Yun
Element: Fire / Lightning

Blade Master is a comprehensive craft sect that uses swords to suppress opponents on the battlefield. Blade Master martial arts are based on fast attack speed and the technique of moving around enemies. Blade Master can dodge and block enemy attacks without stopping, as well as a fierce counterattack with his special moves.

When fighting, Blade Master can switch between two tons of techniques: Basic Pose with a variety of skills to combine attack or Poke Blade with skills that focus on speed. The Blade Master can also summon the Six Gods Sword, allowing this faction to gain the upper hand before the enemy.

Kungfu Master
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin, Gon, Yun
Element: Fire / Wind

The Kungfu Master uses the power of the fist to defeat the opponent, but the true strength of this sect lies in capturing the time of the attack. Taking "of course the reformation" as the mind, the Kungfu Master could catch the opponent's attack, redirect the attack and counterattack in the blink of an eye.

In battle, the Kungfu Master is the one who will control the battle with a series of beautiful and effective moves. Although it is possible to face many opponents at the same time, the Kungfu Master will truly promote the essence of martial arts in one-on-one matches.

Summoner
Difficult:
Tribe: Lyn
Element: Earth / Wind

Summoner is a sect that focuses on controlling and coordinating with the summoned beast to control and defeat the enemy. The Summoned beast will take over, dealing physical damage and drawing enemy attention. Meanwhile, Summoner Master will support his friend with ranged skills: empowering the summoned beasts or attacking both of them.

The greatest strength of Summoners and Summoned Beasts is their ability to independently fight, simultaneously attack and support their allies at the same time. The Summoner may be the ideal school for newbies, but becoming a Summoner is both a process of cultivating skills and strategy.

Soul Master
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin, Gon, Yun
Element: Earth / Ice

Soul Master is a derivative born from the combination of magic and martial arts. Carry on you the power of the great land and the ice. Soul Master has the ability to restore vitality and increase defense for allies and store the power from attacks and explode with great damage.

Soul Master is very strong in fighting. The appearance of the Soul Master in the squad is extremely necessary not only in healing, but also the ability to revive teammates.

Warden
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin, Lyn, Gon
Element: Earth / Ice

Wielding a massive greatsword, the Warden is both capable of absorbing punishment and unleashing it upon its foes. This resilient tank will decimate all those who stand in its way, even if it must spill its own blood to do so.

Zen Archer
Difficult:
Tribe: Jin, Yun
Element: Earth / Ice

Unlike their ranged combat counterparts, the Zen Archer approaches the battlefield with relative calm. As chaos reigns in the middle of combat, Zen Archers can adapt to any scenario while maintaining a harmonic balance of control and precision in the heat of battle.

The Zen Archer can only truly achieve perfection of their skills by sustaining inner peace while in the throes of battle. Their intent is to use nothing more than their bow to unleash a flurry of sharpened destruction, taking down all those who oppose them with absolute composure.
